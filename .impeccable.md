## Design Context

### Users
Event organizers and attendees. Organizers need a reliable, efficient way to manage events, track registrations, and handle check-ins via QR codes. Attendees use the platform to browse events, register, and keep track of their tickets in a personal dashboard. The context is both desktop management (for organizers) and mobile-first on-the-go access (for attendees at physical/hybrid events).

### Brand Personality
Vibrant, professional, and accessible. The combination of energetic purple and orange accents alongside soft rounded corners creates a tone that is welcoming and dynamic, yet trustworthy enough for enterprise-level event management.

### Aesthetic Direction
Modern, clean SaaS aesthetic with a focus on usability. 
- **Theme**: Currently light mode, with white elevated cards on a soft gray background. 
- **Typography**: `Plus Jakarta Sans` for bold, legible headings and `Manrope` for clean, readable body text.
- **Details**: Uses subtle glassmorphism (blurred headers), soft layered shadows, and pill-shaped interactive elements. The primary colors are a vivid purple (`#7c3aed`) and energetic orange (`#f97316`).

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over Clutter**: Keep event details, dates, and calls-to-action (like "Register") immediately visible and unambiguous.
2. **Mobile-First Interactions**: Ensure all touch targets, modals, and navigation drawers feel native and fluid on mobile devices, especially for on-site event check-ins.
3. **Vibrant Accents**: Use the brand's purple and orange purposefully to draw attention to primary actions, rather than overwhelming the interface.
4. **Optimistic & Fast**: Feedback for actions (saving events, registering) should feel instantaneous and smooth.
