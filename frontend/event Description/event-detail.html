Scientific Club Workshop:
AI in Medicine

Tue, Mar 24, 2026 • 09:00 AM
USTHB, Bab Ezzouar, Algiers

About the Event

Join us for a groundbreaking exploration into the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Healthcare. This intensive one-day workshop is designed for students, researchers, and tech enthusiasts eager to understand how machine learning is revolutionizing diagnostics and patient care.

The workshop will cover the fundamentals of neural networks, medical imaging analysis, and the ethical considerations of AI deployment in hospitals. Whether you are a medical student curious about tech or a developer looking to apply skills in a life-saving domain, this event is your gateway.

What you'll learn:

Fundamentals of AI in Diagnostic Imaging
Predictive Analytics for Patient Outcomes
Real-world Case Studies from Algerian Hospitals
